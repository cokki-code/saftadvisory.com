Through personalized consultation and continuous support, we strive to empower the Kuala Terengganu community to build a secure and prosperous future for generations to come.

PruBSN Asas360: 360° Protection from Prenatal to Age 100

PruBSN Asas360 is a flexible, Shariah-compliant investment-linked Takaful plan that offers holistic protection tailored for every stage of life. It is designed to be a comprehensive solution, allowing you to secure protection and maximise your investments within a single, integrated plan.

What It Covers and Offers:

Lifelong Core Protection:

Provides essential coverage for death and Total and Permanent Disability (TPD), with the option for coverage to extend automatically up to age 100.

Medical Protection:

Offers the optional Health360 rider, which provides unlimited medical protection with No Annual Limit and No Lifetime Limit for hospitalisation and outpatient treatments.

Early Life Coverage:

Protection starts as early as 13 gestational weeks of pregnancy. The optional Baby TotalCare rider covers pregnancy complications, congenital conditions, and child development disorders.

Critical Illness Coverage:

Provides financial support for diagnosis of covered Critical Illnesses (up to 166 conditions with the optional Crisis TotalCare rider).

Savings & Rewards:

Includes the Kasih Bonus reward, where you receive a cash bonus every 10 years (1% of Basic Sum Covered). You can also grow your wealth by investing in your choice of 10 Shariah-compliant funds.

Flexibility:

Easily customise your plan by choosing your certificate term and adding optional benefits (up to 20 riders available) to meet your unique needs.

PruBSN Aspirasi: Your All-in-One Savings, Investment, and Protection Plan

PruBSN Aspirasi is a Shariah-compliant Takaful solution designed to help you secure your financial future by combining savings, investment, and essential protection in one plan. It is structured to help you achieve long-term life aspirations, such as building a solid fund for your lifestyle, education, or retirement.

What It Covers and Offers:

Financial Growth & Payouts:
  • Annual Cash Payout: Receive an increasing annual cash payout starting from the end of the 2nd certificate year.
  • Maturity Payout: Get a lump sum cash benefit at the end of your benefit term.
  • Potential Upside Return: Increase your cash value by investing a portion of your contribution into Shariah-compliant funds when you attach a Takaful Saver rider.
Core Protection:
  • Life Coverage: Provides financial protection for you and your family in the event of death or Total and Permanent Disability (TPD).
  • Contributor protect: PruBSN will fully cover the policyholder's contribution, meaning the policy owner doesn't have to pay due to a covered event like disability.
  • Compassionate Benefit: Includes an additional RM3,000 to cover funeral expenses.
Flexibility & Access:
  • Customisable Terms: Choose a contribution payment term (5, 10, or 20 years) and a benefit term (15, 20, 25, or 30 years) that suits your financial goals.
  • Guaranteed Acceptance: No medical checkup is required via the Guaranteed Issuance Offer for the basic plan up to RM300,000 of Basic Sum Covered (subject to age and exclusions).

PruBSN WarisanGold — High-Coverage Legacy Takaful

PruBSN WarisanGold is a forward-thinking, customisable, and Shariah-compliant Takaful plan designed to secure your family's financial future and build a lasting legacy. It provides high-coverage protection and flexible options so you can live worry-free, knowing your loved ones will be protected.

What It Covers and Offers:

  • Affordable High Protection: Substantial coverage for death and TPD, starting from RM350,000 (RM250,000 for juveniles).
  • Accidental Death: Up to 600% of Basic Sum Covered for accidental death, higher for public conveyances or natural disasters.
  • Legacy Enhancement: Protection Enhancer (auto-increasing) and Legacy Booster/Bonus features to grow plan value.
  • Immediate Assistance: Khairat Benefit RM3,000 and Badal Hajj Benefit RM3,000 upon death.
  • Extended Coverage: Option to extend coverage automatically up to age 100.
  • Charity Option: Option to allocate a portion of death benefit for Sadaqah or Wakaf.
  • Flexibility & Investment: Choice of contribution terms and access to 10 Shariah-approved investment-linked funds.
  • Hassle-free Sign-up: High non-medical limit up to RM4 million Basic Sum Covered depending on entry age.

PruBSN Anggun — The Takaful Plan for Every Woman

PruBSN Anggun is a specially designed, Shariah-compliant Takaful plan that focuses exclusively on the protection needs of women at every stage of life. It provides comprehensive coverage for female-specific health conditions, supports mental wellness, and offers cash assistance for major life events.

What It Covers and Offers:

  • Comprehensive Female Protection: Lump sum payouts upon diagnosis of covered female-specific illnesses, including Female Cancer, SLE with severe kidney complications, and Female Carcinoma-In-Situ.
  • Mental Care Benefit: Reimbursement for outpatient psychiatric treatment fees (up to 10% of Basic Sum Covered or RM5,000, whichever is lower) for covered conditions.
  • Mom Care (Optional Rider): Support for future mothers — pregnancy complications, fertility assistance, and protection for child congenital illnesses.
  • Life Stage Cash Rewards: Cash rewards and assistance (up to 9% of Basic Sum Covered) for milestones such as marriage, property purchase, Hajj/Umrah, and retirement.
  • Core Coverage: Female Cancer, SLE, Death and Total and Permanent Disability (TPD) coverage.
  • Affordability & Flexibility: Plans from as low as RM50/month in example scenarios; choose coverage amount and term.

PruBSN Anugerah Max — Maximum Protection, Maximum Flexibility

PruBSN Anugerah Max is a flexible, Shariah-compliant Takaful plan designed for lifelong protection, with coverage that can be extended up to age 100. It is built to offer maximum protection and greater affordability, allowing you to build a comprehensive plan that suits your budget, potentially starting from as low as RM50 per month.

What It Covers and Offers:

  • Comprehensive Medical Coverage: Provides modern hospitalisation and medical treatment coverage through riders like Medic TotalCare. You can optionally boost your limits with Medic BoostCare for an additional RM1,000,000 annual limit.
  • Critical Illness Protection: Choose from various options, including Crisis TotalCare, which offers comprehensive coverage for up to 166 conditions from early to late stages.
  • Core Protection: Secures your family's future with coverage for death and Total and Permanent Disability (TPD).
  • High Customisation: The plan offers superb flexibility and can be customised with options for accidental coverage, income protection, and various contributor riders to ensure your contributions continue if you are unable to pay.
  • Extra Benefits: Includes the EduAchieve Bonus, which grants a cash reward for children who achieve excellent academic results (SPM up to Master's/PhD level). You can also choose to contribute to charity via the Ihsan donation option.
