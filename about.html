ABOUT US

SAFT Advisory Kuala Terengganu 2 is a dedicated takaful agency operating under PruBSN, serving the community since 2006. Our agency is committed to providing financial protection services based on the principles of takaful, helping individuals and families plan their finances, safeguard their future, and achieve greater peace of mind.

Since our establishment, we have focused on supporting the people of Terengganu and beyond with practical, trustworthy solutions for financial security. Our team works closely with clients to understand their needs and offer protection plans that fit their lives and aspirations. We believe that everyone deserves access to reliable financial planning and protection, and we strive to make a positive difference in the lives of our clients every day.
